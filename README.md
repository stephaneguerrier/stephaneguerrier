### Stéphane Guerrier

Associate Professor of Statistics & Data Science at the [University of Geneva](https://www.unige.ch/).

I develop statistical methodology and open-source software for complex data problems in medical and pharmaceutical sciences, robotics, geodesy, and finance. My research is currently supported by four concurrent grants from the [Swiss National Science Foundation](https://data.snf.ch/grants/person/594369) (2024–2028).

**Research interests:** biostatistics · equivalence testing · simulation-based inference · time series & signal processing · reliability & fairness in AI

**Open-source software (R):**
[cTOST](https://github.com/stephaneguerrier/cTOST) — equivalence testing ·
[simts](https://github.com/SMAC-Group/simts) — time series simulation ·
[wv](https://github.com/SMAC-Group/wv) — wavelet variance ·
[gmwm](https://github.com/SMAC-Group/gmwm) / [gmwmx](https://github.com/SMAC-Group/gmwmx) / [gmwmx2](https://github.com/SMAC-Group/gmwmx2) — generalized method of wavelet moments ·
[navigation](https://github.com/SMAC-Group/navigation) — inertial navigation ·
[avar](https://github.com/SMAC-Group/avar) — Allan variance
